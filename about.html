Who are you?

Haven't you took a look at the Home Page? I am the World's BEST Engineer!

Alright, jokes aside, I am an Mechanical Engineering student attending California Univeristy, Fresno. I have 2 more semesters left before graduation. At first I wanted to become an Airline pilot, but then the flight school are too expensive. So I decided to become an Engieer. I wanted to better understand things around me. I love to solve problems and try new things. I like to be creative and solve problems in different ways. I don't believe there is only one right answer; I think there are much more to problem solving than getting the right answer. The process and journey of getting there is what the most rewarding to me. Because I get to meet many different people and enhance my knowledge with their guidance. This long journey to getting my Engineering Degree taught me how to be patient and it builds my perseverance.
