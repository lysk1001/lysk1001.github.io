Engineering

Engineering is not just about problem solving. It's also about solving problems efficiently. As an Engineering student, I've learned that it's best not to re-invent the wheels if I don't have to. In order to save time and resources, it would be best to tweak, or improve the pre-existing model to fit my need. Innovation is also a form of tweaking nature's existing model to fit our need. For example, airplanes are pre-existing models of birds; ships are pre-existing models of fishes. One way or another, Engineering is about solving problems in efficient way that benefit our lifes and those around us.
