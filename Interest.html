Stop Motion Animation

I made this stop motion animation with the speed of 12 frames per second. This mini clip is a part of a larger clips. The thing about stop motion animation is that, for every frame that's basically a picture. So I took about 48 pictures to made this 4 second clip. The camera was static for the entire time.

My 4th PC build

This was the 4th PC I've built so far. It has a RTX 3060 TI graphics card and the CPU cooler is fan cooled. The other one I've build has a RTX 3090 graphics card, and that was liquid cooled. Honestly, the temperature difference wasn't even that significant. I hypothesize that, the PC temperature is more dependent on the ambient temperature than the installed cooler. This PC is equiped with 1TB SSD. I always tried to keep a tidy cable management.

Kerbal Space Program

Yup, another simulation type of game. This is Kerbal Space Program, everything you need to know about rocket science in your back yard. I launched many rockets in order to build this mini space station. From buliding stage rockets to rendezvous docking. First I tried to do it manually, but then I realized I can use 'computerized' mod to do it accurately. Don't feel bad, NASA uses 'computerized' mod to fly their things too.

Flight Simulator
